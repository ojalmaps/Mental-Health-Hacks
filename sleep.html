Make sure to get a good night's rest


Sleep is an essential function that allows your body and mind to recharge, leaving you refreshed and alert when you wake up. Healthy sleep also helps the body remain healthy and stave off diseases. Without enough sleep, the brain cannot function properly. This can impair your abilities to concentrate, think clearly, and process memories. Most adults require between seven and nine hours3 of nightly sleep. Children and teenagers need substantially more sleep, particularly if they are younger than five years of age. Work schedules, day-to-day stressors, a disruptive bedroom environment, and medical conditions can all prevent us from receiving enough sleep. 5 tips to get a good night sleep

  1. Have a sleep schedule and night routine
  2. Avoid using electronics right before bed time
  3. Avoid exercising at night
  4. Make a soothing environment with dim lights
  5. Limit the naps during the day
